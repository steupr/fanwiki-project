Fadrex city was the capitol of the western dominance, and the city where [[Ashweather Cett|Cett]] came to power.  It contained one of [[the Lord Ruler]]'s supply caches, and was one of the most defensible cities in the Final Empire.  It plays a key role in the events of ''[[Mistborn: The Hero of Ages|The Hero of Ages]]'', surviving two sieges before ultimately being destroyed when the world was remade.
