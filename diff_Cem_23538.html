'''Soulcasting''' transforms any material into one of Surgebinder's choice. Transforming something into the pure form of an [[Ten Essences|Essence]] is quite easy.{{ref|b|twok|c|72}} Soulcasting requires the corresponding gemstone for the Essence the Surgebinder is trying to transform into. Soulcasting something into blood, for example, requires a garnet infused with Stormlight.{{cite}} The process may leave the gemstone shattered, although the larger the gemstone the less likely it is for that to happen.{{ref|b|twok|c|15}} The mass of an object remains the same when it is Soulcast.{{ref|b|twok|c|5}} It may not be possible to create gemstones with Soulcasting.{{ref|b|twok|c|33}}
