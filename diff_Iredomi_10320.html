For the majority of his life, he lived in the city of [[Kae]], just outside of [[Elantris]]. Ten years after the [[Shaod]], he underwent the transformation into an [[Elantrian]], a transformation that was, at the time, incomplete. As an Elantrian, he brought peace and civilization to the people living in Elantris, who had devolved into a primitive, bestial society. '''Raoden''' was the first [[Elantrian]] after the [[Shaod]] to successfully harness the power of AonDor. He eventually discovered the key to restoring Elantris, and succeeded in repelling the efforts of [[Wyrn]] in conquering both [[Arelon]] and [[Teod]].
