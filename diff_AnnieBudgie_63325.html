'''Kalyani''' is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s.{{book ref|legion}} She is a linguist who was "created" after Steve flips through a book on Hebrew syntax, grammar, and vocabulary. She is kind and patient and likes to give hugs, and is Indian and sometimes wears a sari. She also may make indian curries for the other aspects, or encourage them to try them, as J.C. mentions her making one for them (which he assumes is Chinese food, since it has rice in it). After asking Stephen multiple times if he can make her husband an aspect too, saying that he is a good photographer, he appears at the mansion where they live.
