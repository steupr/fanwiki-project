Reen and Vin originally lived with their mother, who was the parent they both shared. After the death of Vin's sister by her mother's hands, Reen left with Vin to earn a living in the Skaa underground. The pair travelled with different crews around various towns and cities in the central dominance. When Reen left Vin, they were serving in Camon's crew in Luthadel. Camon claimed that Reen owed him a large sum of money, though the reason behind this is unknown.

Throughout the Mistborn Trilogy, Vin is plagued by Reen's voice which advises her to not put her trust in anyone.  In the events of the Hero of Ages, it is revealed that the voice of Reen is Ruin, speaking to Vin in her half-brother's voice through the medium of her hemalurgically imbued earring.
