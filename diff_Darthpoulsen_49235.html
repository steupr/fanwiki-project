Szeth possessed an Honourblade that was long and thin, edged on both sides. It was smaller than most others. Being bonded to this blade granted Szeth the abilities of a Windrunner. Hence why Szeth had no bond to a spren. At the end of Words of Radiance, Szeth is presented with a new Shardblade that asks him, "Do you want to destroy some evil?" This question, as any reader of Warbreaker knows, is frequently asked by Nightblood, the sentient sword from Warbreaker. It is safe to assume that Szeth is now in possession of Nightblood.
