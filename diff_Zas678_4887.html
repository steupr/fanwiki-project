'''Cenn''' is a young spear man in [[Kaladin]]'s squad. He is young, inexperienced and bears a certain resemblance to Tien. He used to be a chull farmer.
 +
 
 +
[[Dallet]] was his sergeant. He was fifteen years old when he volunteered to enlist in [[Amaram]]'s army, and Kaladin pays money to have him moved to his squad. He expects disipline and order in the army, and is surprised at the chaos. When separated from [[Kaladin]]'s group, Cenn gets stabbed in the leg. Kaladin comes and defends him, then has [[Dallet]] watch over him as healers come to take care of him. Unfortunately, both [[Dallet]] and [[Cenn]] are killed by a trampling [[Shardbearer]], who is possible a [[Ghostblood]].
